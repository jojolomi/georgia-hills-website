Before You Arrive

Visa Policy

Citizens of GCC countries (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain) can enter Georgia visa-free for up to 1 full year.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (Apr-Jun) and Autumn (Sep-Oct) offer the mildest weather. Winter is perfect for skiing in Gudauri.

Halal Food

Halal restaurants are widely available in Tbilisi (especially Marjanishvili) and Batumi. Georgian cuisine is also incredibly vegetarian-friendly.

Currency

The local currency is the Georgian Lari (GEL). Cards are widely accepted in cities, but carry some cash for rural areas like Kazbegi.

Connectivity

Buy a local SIM (Magti or Silknet) at the airport for fast 4G internet. Our premium minivans also come equipped with onboard Wi-Fi.

Safety

Georgia is consistently ranked as one of the safest countries in the world for tourists. Violent crime against travelers is extremely rare.
