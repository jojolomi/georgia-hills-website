Visa & Entry Guide

Planning a trip from the GCC? Georgia offers one of the most welcoming visa policies in the world for citizens of Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, and Bahrain. Here is everything you need to know about entry requirements in 2026.

Visa-Free Entry for GCC Citizens

Citizens of Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, and Bahrain do NOT need a visa to enter Georgia. You can stay in Georgia visa-free for up to 1 full year.

Expatriates holding valid residency visas from GCC countries also enjoy visa-free entry, provided they can show their valid residency card upon arrival.

Required Documents at the Airport

  • A passport valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates.
  • Return or onward flight ticket (occasionally asked).
  • Hotel reservation or proof of accommodation (occasionally asked).
  • Travel health insurance (highly recommended).

Airport Arrival Process

Arriving at Tbilisi International Airport (TBS) or Batumi International Airport (BUS) is straightforward. Passport control is usually quick for GCC citizens. After passing immigration, you can easily buy a local SIM card (Magti or Silknet) right in the arrivals hall.
