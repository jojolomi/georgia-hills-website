Before You Arrive

Key information to help you prepare for your Georgian adventure.

Visa Policy

Citizens of GCC countries (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain) can enter Georgia visa-free for up to 1 year.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (Apr-Jun) and Autumn (Sep-Oct) offer the mildest weather. Winter is perfect for skiing in Gudauri or Bakuriani.

Halal Food

Halal restaurants are widely available in Tbilisi (especially Marjanishvili area) and Batumi. Georgian cuisine also offers many vegetarian dishes.

Currency

The currency is the Georgian Lari (GEL). Cards are widely accepted in cities, but carry cash for rural areas.
