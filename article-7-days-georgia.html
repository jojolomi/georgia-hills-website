7 Days in
Georgia.

A balanced week-long journey through Tbilisi, the Mountains, and the Black Sea coast. Perfect for first-time visitors.

Itinerary Guide

Planning a trip to Georgia? This balanced route covers culture, nature, and relaxation. From the historic baths of Tbilisi to the high peaks of Kazbegi and the pebbled beaches of Batumi.

Day 1-2: Tbilisi Exploration

Start your journey in the vibrant capital. Explore the narrow streets of the Old Town, relax in the famous sulfur baths at Abanotubani, and take the cable car up to Narikala Fortress for panoramic views. In the evening, enjoy a walk along Rustaveli Avenue to feel the city's pulse.

Day 3: The Mountains of Kazbegi

Head north along the Georgian Military Highway. Stop at the Ananuri Fortress and the Russia-Georgia Friendship Monument. Upon reaching Stepantsminda, visit the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church. This day offers some of the most breathtaking mountain views in the country.

Day 4: Relax & Transit

Return from the mountains and take a breather. Use this as a flexible day to visit local markets like the Dry Bridge Market in Tbilisi, or start your journey towards the west. Recharging is key for the second half of the trip to avoid fatigue.

Day 5-6: Batumi & Black Sea

Travel to the Black Sea coast. Batumi offers a contrast to Tbilisi with its modern architecture, long coastal promenade, and beautiful Botanical Garden. Enjoy seafood, visit the Ali & Nino statue, and relax by the sea.

Day 7: Departure

Transfer to the airport. Whether you fly out of Batumi or return to Tbilisi, ensure you leave plenty of buffer time for travel and check-in procedures. Traffic in Tbilisi can be unpredictable.
