The Las Vegas of the Black Sea

Batumi is a vibrant seaside city on the Black Sea coast and the capital of Adjara. Known for its eclectic modern architecture, bustling boulevard, and subtropical climate, it offers a stark contrast to the historical depth of Tbilisi.
