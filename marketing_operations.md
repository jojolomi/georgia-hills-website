## Search Console
1. Add property `https://georgiahills.com`.
2. Verify using `PUBLIC_SEARCH_CONSOLE_VERIFICATION` meta token.
3. Submit `https://georgiahills.com/sitemap.xml`.
4. Track indexing coverage weekly.

## Google Ads (Arabic intent)
- Campaign groups:
  - Family travel
  - Halal travel
  - Luxury honeymoon
  - Driver + transport intent
- Landing pages:
  - `/family-travel-hub-ar.html`
  - `/halal-travel-hub-ar.html`
  - `/booking-ar.html`

Use `PUBLIC_GOOGLE_ADS_ID` to wire conversion tracking in head tags.

## Hotjar and newsletter
- Configure `PUBLIC_HOTJAR_ID` and `PUBLIC_HOTJAR_SV`.
- Configure `PUBLIC_NEWSLETTER_ENDPOINT` for subscriber collection.
- Track KPI events: `newsletter_signup`, `lead_contact_submit`, `social_click`.
