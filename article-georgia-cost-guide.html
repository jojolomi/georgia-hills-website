Budget & Planning

Georgia is renowned for offering incredible value for money. Whether you are seeking a luxurious family vacation or a mid-range getaway, your budget stretches much further here than in Western Europe.

Average Daily Costs

A typical 7-day family trip to Georgia costs between $1,500 and $3,500 total, depending on the level of luxury.

Category Mid-Range (Per Day) Luxury (Per Day)
Hotels $50 - $100 $150 - $350+
Food & Dining $20 - $40 $50 - $100+
Private Driver $70 - $90 $100 - $120 (Minivan)

Why a Private Driver Saves Money

It might seem counterintuitive, but hiring a full-time private driver with Georgia Hills is often cheaper than renting a car or taking multiple taxis. Our daily rate covers the driver, fuel, parking, and tolls. You avoid rental deposits, expensive insurance, and the stress of navigating mountain roads.
